About Vision Scout

Our Mission

To modernize the Scouting experience for members aged 7-30 by providing sustainable uniforms, personalized activities, and leadership development opportunities.

Our Vision

Vision Scout is designed to support Profitis Scout Teams with innovative tools, customizable experiences, and a platform that connects Scouts globally.

Our Goals

  • Launch an engaging online store with sustainable uniforms
  • Implement an AI-driven clothing suggestion system
  • Partner with eco-friendly brands for all products
  • Reach high user retention rates through community engagement

Key Features

  • Personalized clothing recommendations
  • Activity organizer and event planning tools
  • Mobile app integration for on-the-go access
  • Community forums for Scout discussions
  • Sustainable, eco-friendly products

Community Focus

We believe in empowering Scouts through teamwork, environmental awareness, and outdoor activities. Our platform supports both indoor and outdoor adventures.
